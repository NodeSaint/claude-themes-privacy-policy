Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Claude Themes is a browser extension that customises the visual appearance of claude.ai. It is developed by nuvix.studio.

Data Collection

Claude Themes does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

Data Storage

The extension stores your theme preferences (colour values and settings) locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. This data never leaves your browser.

What the Extension Does

Claude Themes injects a CSS stylesheet into claude.ai pages to override visual properties such as colours and fonts. It does not:

  • Read, access, or modify your conversations with Claude
  • Interact with Claude's functionality in any way
  • Access any data on the page beyond applying visual styles
  • Communicate with any external server
  • Track your browsing activity

Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your theme preferences locally on your device
  • activeTab — required for the extension popup to interact with the current tab
  • scripting — injects the CSS theme override into claude.ai pages
  • Host permission (claude.ai) — limits the extension to only operate on claude.ai, no other websites

Third Parties

Claude Themes does not share data with any third parties. There is no data to share.
